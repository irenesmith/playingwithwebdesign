Essential Elements

Certain pages and sections have become standard for websites.

Depending on the type of website we are visiting, we've come to expect certain common features.

This tutorial was written for The Tangled Web but it has been updated to reflect today's web standards.

Does it surprise you that what people expect to find on a website has changed somewhat over the years? I'm more surprised at how much has stayed the same. At least, the content people expect to find hasn't changed that much. But the way we present it does. I'll talk more about that in the Page Essentials tutorial.

Of course, how you present them is where the creativity comes in.

Let's take a look at some of the elements that every website should have and then we'll also look at those elements that every page in your site should have.

None of the ideas I'm presenting are hard and fast rules. Once you are familiar with the basics, you can do what you want.
